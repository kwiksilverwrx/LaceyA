Inspired by I-club.com, a group of Subaru enthusiast came together and created a weekly meet with the occasional coastal drives.

Everyone is welcome to join our drives and meets. We enjoy all makes and models of powerful vehicles.

Our local meets are typically on Saturday evenings in the lovely town of Novato, CA.
